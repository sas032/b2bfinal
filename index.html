Bit2Byte is a community of motivated,enthusiastic students about programming, develepment, cracking and pentesting and all other aspects of computer science and information technology.

 

The Coding Club orgainzes regular coding contests, hackathons, code golf events on popular coding platforms in order to nurture the programming knowledge in students and make them more inclined towards development.  Lectures on algorithms and data structures are organised to make the students aware of the theoretical aspects of programming too.

 

Just like short study breaks are important, similarly other fun events are also organised where FIFA,Counter Strike, NFS are the most popular in the campus.

 

 

 

Bit2Byte always operates without any rest making sure the students enjoy being a part of it and learn something new each and every day.
